BIO

This is a bio for the rock band CONDITIONER. Having not written a bio, they opted instead to use this paragraph of text for the purposes of filling up website space. You see, website space needed to be filled to give the illusion of content. Which is the same thing an artist bio does: fill up space and give the impression of importance. We are going to take a pause here for a picture.

CONDITIONER of course are back after the picture, this time to talk about their accolades and the cool things they have done. Interesting touring bands they've opened for. Places they've been and things they've seen. You look for bands you're familiar with but nothing rings a bell.
